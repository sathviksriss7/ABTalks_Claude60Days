🧮 How the score works

Air Score = 100 − (AQI ÷ 3). Water Score uses 2025 state-level CPCB/NITI Aayog water quality indices. Overall = average of both, weighted equally.

🎯 Grade bands

A 90–100 · B 80–89 · C 70–79 · D 60–69 · F below 60. Hair/Skin risk grades follow Low→A, Moderate→C, High→F.

              ⚠️ This dashboard is for general environmental-awareness purposes only and is not medical advice. PM2.5/PM10 values for 8 of 9 cities are modeled from live US-AQI readings using the standard EPA breakpoint conversion (validated against Nellore's directly-reported PM2.5 of 33.7 µg/m³, model output 34.0 µg/m³). PM10 is estimated using a typical Indian urban PM10:PM2.5 ratio of 1.8 where direct station data was unavailable at capture time. Water Quality Scores use state-level indices as a city proxy, since real-time city-level water quality monitoring is not publicly available the way AQI is. AQI fluctuates hour to hour — treat this as a directional snapshot, not a certified reading.

              🧪 Data Quality Notes
              ✅ 9/9 cities validated, 0 missing values 🔁 Cross-checked against 2 independent monitors 📐 PM2.5 model error ≈ 0.9% vs ground truth
